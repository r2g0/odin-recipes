Lasagna

Description

To make a classic lasagna, start by preheating your oven to 375째F (190째C). In a large skillet, cook 1 pound of ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat, then add 1 chopped onion, 2 cloves of minced garlic, and 2 cups of marinara sauce. Simmer for about 10 minutes. In a separate bowl, mix 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Cook 9 lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.

In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, and then a layer of the ricotta mixture. Repeat the layers, finishing with noodles topped with the remaining meat sauce and 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden. Let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

For the Lasagna:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: In a skillet, brown 1 pound of ground beef or Italian sausage with 1 chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves. Drain excess fat, then stir in 2 cups of marinara sauce and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  2. Mix the Cheese Filling: In a bowl, combine 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Cook 9 lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.
  3. Assemble and Bake: In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer the meat sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, finishing with noodles and the remaining meat sauce topped with 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese. Cover with foil and bake at 375째F (190째C) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
